Gari

Description

This is a recipe on how to prepare gari soakings the Ghanaian student way.

Ingredients

  • 250g of fine gari
  • Roasted groundnut
  • Non-diary creamer or milk
  • Cold Water
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Pour to the gari into your serving bowl.
  2. Add 2 spoons of sugar.
  3. Pour a considerable amount of the roasted groundnut into the mix.
  4. Mix milk with the gari if the milk is powdered, or soak it with creamer milk
  5. Add small amount of water, making sure it's not too much to become light
  6. Stir and serve
