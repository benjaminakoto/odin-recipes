Indomie

Description

This is a recipe on how to prepare Indomie instant noodles the Ghanaian student way.

Ingredients

  • 1 packs of Indomie Instant Noodles Belle Fu
  • Onions
  • 2 eggs
  • Sausages
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Bell pepper

Steps

  1. Start with your vegetables, wash and cut the onions, carrots and cabbage to small pieces. Their quantity should be to your taste.
  2. Bring 500ml of water to boil.
  3. Unpack the noodles and drop it into the boiling water.
  4. Add a small amount of the spice in the pack to the boiling water.
  5. Wait for about 5 minutes for noodles to soften enough. The longer it stays in the water the softer it becomes.
  6. Drain all the water from the noodles.
  7. Preheat your frying pan a bit with some oil to layer the base of the pan.
  8. Add your onions to the pan and stir.
  9. Crack your eggs into the pan and sprinkle some spice unto it.
  10. Add your noodles and stir to mix.
  11. Add your vegetable and continue to stir.
  12. Add some spices to your taste.
  13. Your food is ready to serve.
